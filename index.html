Our Story

Born in Mumbai,
Loved Across India

Founded in 2008 by Priya Sharma, Luxe Studio began as a single boutique salon in Bandra, Mumbai. With a vision rooted in artistry and a commitment to making every Indian feel like royalty, we grew — one happy client at a time.

Today, we operate 12 premium locations across Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Ahmedabad — each a blend of local culture and global aesthetics.
